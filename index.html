About Me

I am associated with Computing and Vision Lab of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi, India as senior research fellow under the supervision of Head of the Department Prof. Rajeev Srivastava. My research area focuses on Digital Image Forgery Detection which is sub discipline of Digital Image Processing and Computer Vision. For my research purpose I use conventional machine learning and deep learning methods.

I have also professional experience of 1+ year in web based application development using ASP.NET, MVC & SQL. I have been associated with Sun Integrated Technologies and Applications as assistant programmer.

Throughout my academics, I actively participated in curricular activities and achieved many awards. Currently I am member of department post graduate committee and department representative of training and placement cell.

During my free time, I love to see new places, experience the beauty and food of different regions. I also love to listen music and play bamboo flute.
