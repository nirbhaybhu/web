About Me

I am working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology Jais, Amethi India. My research area focuses on Person Re-Identifiction using Deep Learning Techniques is sub discipline of Digital Image Processing and Computer Vision. For my research purpose I use conventional machine learning and deep learning methods.

During my free time, I love to explore new places, experience the beauty and food of different regions. I also love to listen music and play games.
